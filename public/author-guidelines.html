Author Guidelines

These guidelines simplify the manuscript submission process for The Global Science. ISSN is currently Pending. The journal is Open Access.

Scope: Multidisciplinary License: CC BY 4.0

1. Recommended article structure

  • Title — clear and concise.
  • Authors — full name, affiliation, ORCID (optional).
  • Abstract — 150–250 words.
  • Keywords — 5–7 items.
  • Introduction — problem statement and objectives.
  • Methods / Materials — methodology (as appropriate).
  • Results — findings.
  • Discussion — analysis and comparison.
  • Conclusion — conclusions and recommendations.
  • References — bibliography.

2. Formatting requirements

  • File: PDF (required). Supplementary files (tables/datasets) are optional.
  • Language: Uzbek / English (or mixed, depending on the topic). Providing the abstract in English is recommended.
  • Plagiarism: authors are fully responsible. All citations must be clearly indicated.
  • Figures: must be readable, with captions and numbered labels.

3. References (recommended format)

APA 7 or IEEE is recommended. The key requirement is consistency. If a DOI is available, it is recommended to include it.

4. Submission workflow

  • Register → Login → Submit.
  • Manuscript status: submitted → screening → (review/revision) → accepted → published.
  • The editorial office will contact the author through the platform.

5. Author responsibilitiesning majburiyatlari

  • Original ish (yoki aniq cite bilan).
  • Conflict of interest bo'lsa ko'rsatish.
  • Authorlar ro'yxati va hissasi adolatli.
