Submit Your Manuscript

The Global Science accepts original research articles, reviews, and short communications. Manuscripts are submitted directly to the editorial office via email.

Send Manuscript via Email

Submission Guide

  1. Click the “Send Manuscript via Email” button above.
  2. Your email application (Gmail, Outlook, Mail) will open automatically.
  3. Attach your manuscript file in PDF format.
  4. Write author details (name, affiliation, contact email).
  5. Send the email to the editorial office.
  6. You will receive a confirmation reply after editorial review.
